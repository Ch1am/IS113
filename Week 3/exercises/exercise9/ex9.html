Exercise 9

Practice two CommonJS patterns that mirror ES Modules:

  • Renaming a named import during destructuring.
  • Importing the entire module object.

Tasks

  1. In shapes.js, create and export functions areaCircle(r), areaSquare(s), and areaTriangle(b, h).
  2. In app.js, import areaCircle but rename it to circleArea.
  3. Also import the entire module as shapes.
  4. Log results of circleArea(3), shapes.areaSquare(4), and shapes.areaTriangle(3, 4).
